Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This policy explains how noteghost ("we", "the app") handles your data. We believe in being honest and clear about what we collect and why.

What we collect

When you use noteghost, we collect the following information to make the app work:

  • Account info: We use Google and Apple for sign-in. This gives us your email address, display name, and profile picture.
  • Profile info: You choose a username. We also track your virtual points balance and "solve count." Points have no monetary value and are only for app features.
  • Audio clips: When you record a song you want identified, we upload that audio clip to our storage.
  • Posts: This includes the titles, descriptions, and genre tags you add to your audio clips.
  • Guesses: Any YouTube links or notes you submit to help others identify their songs.
  • App activity: We keep track of follows, post views, and reports you submit (including the reason and any optional notes).

Third-party services

We use a few trusted services to help run noteghost:

  • Supabase: We use Supabase to store our database, manage user accounts, and host audio files. Your data may be processed on servers in the United States and the European Union.
  • ACRCloud: When you record a clip, the audio is sent to ACRCloud servers. They analyze the fingerprint of the audio to see if they can identify the song. This only happens when you record a clip for identification.
  • Google & Apple: These services handle the sign-in process. Their own privacy policies apply to the information they share with us.

How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Identify songs using audio fingerprinting.
  • Build your social feed and suggest posts you might like.
  • Manage the virtual points economy.
  • Keep the community safe through our reporting system.

Your rights

  • Account Deletion: If you want to delete your account and data, please contact us at admin@noteghost.app. We will process your request as soon as possible.
  • Data Requests: You can contact us at any time to ask what data we have about you.

Updates

If we make major changes to how we handle your data, we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date at the top.
